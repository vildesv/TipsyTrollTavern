Discover Oakenshire

Nestled deep within ancient oak forests and drifting mist, Oakenshire is a village where magic and everyday life quietly intertwine. Lantern-lit streets guide travelers, adventurers, and curious souls through a place that always feels alive with stories. At the heart of it all lies the lively Tipsy Troll Tavern — where most journeys begin, and some never quite end.
