# 🧌 Tipsy Troll Tavern

Welcome to **Tipsy Troll Tavern**, a magical, interactive website that immerses you in a whimsical fantasy world. Explore the tavern exterior, step inside for warm lights and cozy vibes, or uncover hidden corners of the world… ✨

---

## 🏠 Pages Overview

The website now contains **five enchanting pages**, each with unique visuals, effects, and music:

---

### 1️⃣ Homepage (`index.html`)
- **Description:** The exterior of the tavern, featuring flickering lanterns, glowing window light, and bubbles rising from the tavern sign.  
- **Interactivity:** Hover over the door to reveal the “Enter tavern” text. If you look closely, you might notice something hidden in the scene…  
- **Music:** [Ambient-music by giangallegos](https://freesound.org/s/812170/) – Creative Commons 0  

---

### 2️⃣ Tavern (`tavern.html`)
- **Description:** The cozy interior filled with warm light, flickering lamps, magical smoke, and interactive popups.  
- **Interactivity:** Click on objects and characters to reveal dialogue and hidden details.  
- **Music:** [My Tavern 1.wav by ralexpdx](https://freesound.org/s/321220/) – Attribution 4.0  

---

### 3️⃣ Backroom (`hiddenHand.html`)
- **Description:** The secretive **Hidden Hand** room, filled with living candles, blue magical glow, and mysterious interactive elements.  
- **Interactivity:** Click hidden areas to open popups or leave messages stored in localStorage.  
- **Music:** [Pirate Tavern by brunoboselli](https://freesound.org/s/695295/) – Creative Commons 0  

---

### 4️⃣ Oakenshire (`village.html`)
- **Description:** The nearby village of Oakenshire, where travelers pass through winding cobblestone streets and stories linger in the air.  
- **Interactivity:** Explore points of interest across the village.  
- **Music:** [STILL DRE | Medieval Bardcore Version | Dr Dre & Snoop Dogg vs Beedle the Bardcore](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L1Uvr5v8IOE)

---

### 5️⃣ Elderwood Forest (`forest.html`)
- **Description:** A vast, ancient forest filled with mist, towering trees, and quiet, lingering magic.  
- **Interactivity:** Navigate through the forest atmosphere and discover hidden details.  
- **Music:** [Sound Effect by Alexander Jauk from Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/nature-calm-nature-sounds-196258/)

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML** – Structure for all pages and content  
- **CSS** – Visual effects such as glow, smoke, flickering lights, bubbles, and animations  
- **JavaScript** – Interactivity including popups, audio controls, hover effects, and localStorage features  

---

## ✨ Features

- Immersive fantasy environments across multiple locations  
- Ambient music unique to each page  
- Interactive elements (hover effects, clickable objects, popups)  
- Local storage functionality for saving hidden messages  
- Animated visuals (lighting, smoke, glow, particles, and more)
